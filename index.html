Everything your team needs

From room-level condition ratings to district-wide FCI reports — FacilitiesDB covers the full assessment lifecycle.

Condition Inventory

Capture FCI scores, deficiency costs, and component conditions at the building, room, and system level. Fully configurable terminology per organization.

AI-Powered Insights

Ask plain-English questions about your portfolio. Get instant summaries, cost projections, and prioritized recommendations powered by a self-hosted AI model.

Multi-District Scale

Each organization gets its own isolated tenant — separate data, branding, and user access. Deploy once, serve any number of districts or property portfolios.

Energy Tracking

Monitor electricity, gas, and EUI per square foot alongside condition data. Identify high-cost buildings and track improvements over time.

Role-Based Access

Zone managers see their buildings. District administrators see everything. Granular group-based access control keeps sensitive data in the right hands.

Audit & Reporting

Full audit trail on every change. Export FCA condition reports, energy summaries, and portfolio dashboards to CSV. Print-ready layouts included.
